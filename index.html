WEB

The World Wide Web(WWW, W3) is a global information space where people can share information through computersconnected to the Internet. It is often referred to simply as the Web. The term is often synonymous with the Internet, but strictly speaking, it is a different concept. The Web is just one service that operates on the Internet, such as e-mail. Since 1993, however, the Web has occupied an absolute position in the Internet structure.It also tells how to transfer pictures and characters using http protocols, hypertext, HTMLformats, etc. on the Internet.
